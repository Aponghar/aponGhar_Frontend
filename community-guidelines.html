Community Guidelines

AponGhar Platform Standards

Our Goal

AponGhar is built to create a safe, respectful, and trustworthy environment for guests and property owners.

By using AponGhar, you agree to follow these guidelines.

Respect Others

Users must:

  • Treat others respectfully.
  • Communicate professionally.
  • Avoid harassment or discrimination.

Honest Listings

Property owners must:

  • Use accurate descriptions.
  • Upload genuine property photos.
  • Keep pricing and availability updated.

Misleading listings may be removed.

Honest Reviews

Reviews should:

  • Reflect genuine experiences.
  • Be respectful.
  • Be factual.

Fake reviews may be removed.

Safety

Guests and owners must comply with local laws.

Activities involving:

  • Fraud
  • Violence
  • Illegal substances
  • Harassment

are prohibited.

Account Integrity

Do not:

  • Create fake accounts.
  • Share login credentials.
  • Manipulate reviews.
  • Abuse promotions.

Enforcement

AponGhar may:

  • Remove content.
  • Suspend accounts.
  • Restrict access.
  • Permanently ban users.

for violations of these guidelines.
