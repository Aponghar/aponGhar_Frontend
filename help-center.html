Frequently Asked Questions

For Guests

How do I book a property?

  1. Search for a property.
  2. Select your dates.
  3. Choose your room.
  4. Complete payment.
  5. Receive booking confirmation.

What payment methods are supported?

We currently support:

  • UPI
  • Debit Cards
  • Credit Cards
  • Net Banking
  • Supported Wallets

Can I cancel my booking?

Yes.

  • More than 24 hours before check-in → 100% refund
  • Within 24 hours before check-in → 50% refund

How do I get my refund?

Refunds are generally processed to your original payment method within 1–7 business days.

Can I leave a review?

Yes.

Only verified guests who complete their stay can leave reviews.

For Property Owners

How do I list my property?

Create an owner account and submit your property details for review.

When will my property go live?

After approval by the AponGhar team.

How do withdrawals work?

Owners can request withdrawals anytime through their dashboard.

No minimum withdrawal amount is required.

How long do withdrawals take?

Typically 1–3 business days.

  • Basic Plan
  • Premium Plan
