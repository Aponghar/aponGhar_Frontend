1. Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how AponGhar uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit our website and use our services.

By continuing to use AponGhar, you consent to the use of cookies as described in this policy.

2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website.

They help websites remember information about your visit and improve your browsing experience.

3. How We Use Cookies

AponGhar uses cookies to:

  • Keep users logged in
  • Remember preferences
  • Improve website performance
  • Analyze visitor behavior
  • Enhance security
  • Prevent fraudulent activity
  • Improve user experience

4. Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly.

Examples include:

  • Login sessions
  • Security features
  • Authentication systems

Without these cookies, certain services may not work.

Performance Cookies

These cookies help us understand:

  • Website traffic
  • Popular pages
  • User interactions

This information helps us improve platform performance.

Functional Cookies

These cookies remember user preferences such as:

  • Language settings
  • Saved preferences
  • Personalized experiences

Analytics Cookies

Analytics cookies may collect information about:

  • Device type
  • Browser type
  • Session duration
  • Visitor behavior

This helps us improve our services.

6. Managing Cookies

  • View cookies
