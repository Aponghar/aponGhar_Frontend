Cancellation Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

1. Introduction

This Cancellation Policy outlines the rules and procedures for canceling bookings made through AponGhar.

By making a booking through AponGhar, users agree to this Cancellation Policy.

2. Guest-Initiated Cancellations

Guests may cancel eligible bookings through their AponGhar account or by contacting support.

Refund eligibility depends on when the cancellation is made.

3. Cancellation More Than 24 Hours Before Check-in

If a booking is canceled at least 24 hours before the scheduled check-in time:

Guest Receives

  • 100% refund of the booking amount.

No cancellation penalty will apply.

4. Cancellation Within 24 Hours Before Check-in

If a booking is canceled less than 24 hours before the scheduled check-in time:

Guest Receives

  • 50% refund of the booking amount.

The remaining amount may be retained as a cancellation charge.

5. No-Show Policy

A booking is considered a "No-Show" when:

  • The guest fails to arrive.
  • No cancellation request is submitted.
  • The property cannot contact the guest.

In such situations:

  • The booking may be marked as completed.
  • Refund eligibility may be denied.

6. Property-Initiated Cancellations

If a property owner cancels a confirmed booking:

AponGhar may:

  • Issue a full refund to the guest.
  • Assist the guest in finding an alternative property.
  • Review the owner's account for policy violations.

Repeated cancellations by owners may result in penalties or suspension.

7. Platform-Initiated Cancellations

AponGhar may cancel bookings when:

  • Fraud is suspected.
  • Payment verification fails.
  • Platform abuse is detected.
  • Legal or safety concerns arise.

Refund decisions will be made based on the circumstances.

8. Force Majeure

Certain events may affect bookings, including:

  • Natural disasters
  • Floods
  • Earthquakes
  • Government restrictions
  • Public emergencies
  • Transportation shutdowns

In such cases, AponGhar may evaluate cancellation and refund requests individually.

9. Changes to Bookings

Modification requests such as:

  • Date changes
  • Guest count changes
  • Property changes

are subject to:

  • Property availability
  • Owner approval
  • Updated pricing

AponGhar cannot guarantee booking modifications.

10. Refund Processing

Eligible refunds will be processed according to the AponGhar Refund Policy.

Processing times may vary depending on:

  • Payment provider
  • Banking systems
  • Payment method

11. Contact Information

For cancellation assistance, please contact:

AponGhar

Website: https://aponghar.in

Support Email: support.aponghar@gmail.com

Please provide:

  • Booking ID
  • Registered Email Address
  • Cancellation Request Details

for quicker support.
