Guest Agreement

Last Updated: June 2026

This Guest Agreement governs the use of AponGhar by travelers and guests.

By creating an account, making a booking, or using AponGhar services, you agree to this Agreement.

1. Eligibility

Guests must:

  • Be at least 18 years old.
  • Provide accurate information.
  • Use the platform lawfully.

2. Account Responsibilities

Guests are responsible for:

  • Maintaining account security.
  • Protecting passwords and OTPs.
  • Keeping profile information accurate.

Guests are responsible for activity occurring through their accounts.

3. Booking Rules

Guests agree to:

  • Provide correct booking details.
  • Respect property rules.
  • Follow check-in and check-out procedures.
  • Communicate honestly with property owners.

Booking confirmation depends on successful payment processing.

4. Payments

Guests may pay using:

  • UPI
  • Debit Cards
  • Credit Cards
  • Net Banking
  • Supported Wallets

All payments are processed through authorized payment partners.

5. Wallet Usage

AponGhar wallet balances:

  • Can be used toward bookings.
  • Cannot be withdrawn as cash.
  • Cannot be transferred between users.

Wallet misuse may result in account restrictions.

6. Reviews and Ratings

Only verified guests who complete a stay may leave reviews.

Reviews must:

  • Be truthful.
  • Be respectful.
  • Reflect actual experiences.

Guests may not submit:

  • Fake reviews
  • Offensive content
  • Defamatory content
  • Misleading information

7. Cancellations and Refunds

Refund eligibility follows the AponGhar Refund Policy and Cancellation Policy.

Current standard policy:

  • More than 24 hours before check-in → Full refund.
  • Within 24 hours before check-in → 50% refund.

Additional conditions may apply.

8. Guest Conduct

Guests must not:

  • Damage property.
  • Disturb other guests.
  • Engage in illegal activity.
  • Harass staff or owners.
  • Misuse platform services.

Property owners may take appropriate action where necessary.

9. Property Accuracy

AponGhar works to maintain accurate listings.

However:

  • Property descriptions are provided by owners.
  • Availability may change.
  • Certain amenities may vary.

Guests should review property details before booking.

10. Limitation of Liability

AponGhar operates as a booking platform connecting guests and property owners.

AponGhar is not responsible for:

  • Property conditions
  • Owner conduct
  • Guest conduct
  • Personal losses
  • Travel disruptions

To the maximum extent permitted by law.

11. Account Suspension

Accounts may be suspended for:

  • Fraudulent activity
  • Abuse of the platform
  • False information
  • Review manipulation
  • Policy violations
