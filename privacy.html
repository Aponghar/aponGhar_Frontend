3. Information We Collect

3.1 Personal Information

When you create an account, make a booking, list a property, or contact us, we may collect:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Mobile phone number
  • Date of birth (if provided)
  • Address details
  • Government-issued identification information when required

  • Property ownership details (for owners)

3.2 Booking Information

When you make a booking, we may collect:

  • Check-in date
  • Check-out date
  • Number of guests
  • Property selected
  • Booking history
  • Special requests

3.3 Payment Information

Payments are processed through trusted third-party payment providers such as Razorpay.

AponGhar does not store complete credit card, debit card, UPI PIN, or banking credentials.

We may receive:

  • Payment status
  • Transaction ID
  • Payment amount
  • Refund information

3.4 Wallet Information

We may store:

  • Wallet balance
  • Wallet transaction history

  • Promotional rewards

User wallet balances can only be used for bookings on AponGhar.

3.5 Property Owner Information

For property owners, we may collect:

  • Property information
  • Room information
  • Pricing details
  • Bank account details for payouts
  • Withdrawal requests
  • Business-related information

3.6 Technical Information

We may automatically collect:

  • IP address
  • Browser type
  • Device information
  • Operating system
  • App version
  • Usage activity
  • Cookies and analytics data

We use information to:

  • Create and manage accounts
  • Process bookings

  • Prevent fraud and abuse
