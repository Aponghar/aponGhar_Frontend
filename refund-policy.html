Refund Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

1. Introduction

This Refund Policy explains how refunds are processed for bookings made through AponGhar.

By making a booking through AponGhar, you agree to this Refund Policy.

2. Eligibility for Refunds

Refund eligibility depends on the cancellation timing and booking status.

Users may be eligible for a refund if:

  • A booking is canceled according to the Cancellation Policy.
  • A duplicate payment occurs.
  • A payment is successfully charged but no booking is created.
  • A technical issue results in an incorrect charge.
  • AponGhar determines that a refund is appropriate under special circumstances.

3. Standard Refund Rules

Cancellation More Than 24 Hours Before Check-in

Guests are eligible for:

100% Refund of the Booking Amount

provided that the booking is canceled at least 24 hours before the scheduled check-in time.

Cancellation Within 24 Hours Before Check-in

Guests are eligible for:

50% Refund of the Booking Amount

when cancellation occurs within 24 hours of the scheduled check-in time.

4. Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds may not be issued when:

  • A guest fails to arrive at the property (No-Show).
  • False information was provided during booking.
  • Fraudulent activity is detected.
  • Platform policies have been violated.
  • Payment disputes are initiated through unauthorized methods.

AponGhar reserves the right to investigate such cases.

5. Duplicate Payments

If a user is charged more than once for the same booking:

  • The duplicate amount will be reviewed.
  • Eligible duplicate payments will be refunded.

Users should contact support with transaction details.

6. Failed Bookings

If payment is completed but the booking is not successfully created due to a technical issue:

  • The payment may automatically reverse.
  • If automatic reversal does not occur, a manual refund may be processed.

7. Refund Processing Time

Approved refunds are generally processed within:

1–7 business days

depending on:

  • Banking partner
  • Payment method
  • Razorpay processing timelines
  • Issuing bank policies

Some banks may require additional time.

8. Refund Method

Refunds will generally be issued through the original payment method used for the booking.

Examples include:

  • UPI
  • Credit Card
  • Debit Card
  • Net Banking
  • Digital Wallet

Alternative refund methods may be used where required.

9. Wallet Transactions

Wallet balances used during a booking may be returned to the user's AponGhar wallet where applicable.

Wallet balances:

  • Are non-transferable.
  • Cannot be withdrawn as cash.
  • May only be used for eligible bookings on AponGhar.

10. Special Circumstances

AponGhar may review refund requests involving:

  • Natural disasters
  • Government restrictions
  • Property closure
  • Safety concerns
  • Force majeure events

Additional documentation may be requested.
